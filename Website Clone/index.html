WHO WE ARE?

BTS is a consultancy specializing in the people side of strategy. For over three decades we’ve been designing powerful experiences that have a profound and lasting impact on businesses and their people. We help the world’s leading companies turn strategy into results.

Our next-generation approach combines deep business knowledge with transformational development to help your people and your company evolve together. We equip leaders for tomorrow. We inspire new ways of thinking. We build critical capabilities. We make strategy personal.

In short: We unlock human and business success.
